Our Philosophy

Since 2010, Boule Bakery has been dedicated to the craft of making the finest baked goods around. Owner Linda Pan is a graduate of the Institute of Culinary Education and has been a baker ever since she got an Easy Bake Oven as a child. We use only the highest-quality flours, sugar, cage-free eggs, and hormone-free dairy in our goods. Visit us for your next special occasion!
