How It Works

Campus Perks aims to serve as your school's community enhancer to help you get the most out of your educational experience. Through a collective effort, we want to help you to be better at what you do and improve your experience on campus to help you grow as an individual. Our online platform connects you to your fellow classmates to share knowledge through the following services:

Peer Tutoring

Get tutored in your subject by peers who took the same class and professor. Get insights on their homework and exams, all while getting to know your peers better. Make a quick buck by teaching fellow peers between classes. Set your own rate and schedule appointments at your own convenience.

Note Sharing

Take a snap of past exams, homework solutions and papers, and post it on Campus Perks helping out your peers. Like, share and comment on their notes and learn from them through the comments section.

On-Campus Events

Create and invite your peers to join your On-Campus events. Attend other events that are posted and get to know like-minded individuals.
