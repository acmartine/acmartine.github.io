About Me

I am a UX/UI designer with a love for front-end development. My background includes Startup Institute New York's Web Design Track, a BA in Psychology from Bryn Mawr College, and experience in non-profit development and education.

When not at my computer, you can find me staring at the night sky, mixing new bath & body products in my kitchen, nose deep in a book on the subway, or biking around the city.

Currently, I am available for freelance and full-time opportunities. Interested in working with me? Contact me for more information!
