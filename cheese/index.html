Our Story

Winner of Boston's 2009 Cheese Grilldown Contest, Bryan was born and raised in East Boston. Over time, he began to experiment with different combinations of ingredients, ultimately leaving his day job in video production to start Cheese Sammies and share his creations with the city. Stop by Cheese Sammies and make your lunch hour a little more fun!
