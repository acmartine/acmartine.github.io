About

Born and raised in East Boston, Bryan has been a grilled cheese aficionado since his younger days. Over time, he began to experiment with different combinations of ingredients, ultimately winning Boston's 5th annual Cheese Grilldown contest in 2009. A few years ago, he quit his day job in video production to start Cheese Sammies and share his creations with the city. Stop by Cheese Sammies and make your lunch hour a little more fun!
